With more than 114,000 people currently living with HIV/AIDS in New York City and more than 50,000 new HIV infections nationally each year, the fight against HIV/AIDS is not over.

GMHC, one of the world's most comprhensive HIV/AIDS service organizations, serves nearly 9,000 clients with an unmatched breadth of HIV/AIDS services including HIV testing and counseling, case management and housing assistance, mental health services, legal assistance, benefits advocacy, food and nutrition services, and job training and placement.
